Project1:FireGuard drone

The Fireguard Drone is an innovative and advanced drone system designed specifically for extinguishing fires.

This project integrates cutting-edge technology to provide rapid response and effective firefighting capabilities, ensuring safety and efficiency.

The Fireguard Drone project aims to revolutionize firefighting by providing a versatile and reliable tool

that can operate in environments where traditional methods may be too dangerous or ineffective.

Project2:CryptoLend

CryptoLend is an innovative and advanced lending system based on blockchain.

It removes the pain and time involved in traditional lending systems by removing intermediaries.

Hence creating a peer-to-peer network for lending and borrowing.

In this system, lending and borrowing are managed by smart contracts, which execute actions when certain conditions are met.

This means that the contract will take immediate action to pay back the loan if the borrower does not pay back in the specified time.
